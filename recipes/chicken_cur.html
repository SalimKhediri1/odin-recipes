Chicken Curry

Description

This chicken curry is rich, flavorful, and full of aromatic spices. A comforting meal that goes great with rice or bread.

It’s an easy one-pot dish that’s ideal for weeknight dinners or family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast or thighs, cut into cubes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onion, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in curry powder and tomato paste. Cook 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  5. Simmer uncovered for 20 minutes until sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with coriander.
  7. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
